This UI design for **CoreBrain** follows a **"Futuristic Professional"** aesthetic—think *Linear* meets *Iron Man’s Jarvis*. We use a "Glassmorphic" design (blurred backgrounds), high-contrast typography, and subtle neon accents (Electric Blue/Violet) against a deep obsidian background.

---

### **Navigation Architecture: The "Command Orbit"**
Instead of a standard bottom bar, we use a **Floating Dock** (similar to iOS/macOS) that can be hidden. At the center is the **"Core" Button**, which opens the Global AI Search/Action Command.

---

### **Screen 1: The Nexus (Dashboard)**
*The central nervous system of your day.*

*   **Header:** 
    *   **"Good Morning, [Name]"** in thin, wide typography. 
    *   **AI Pulse:** A small, breathing circular gradient in the top right. If it’s glowing, the local model is "dreaming" (indexing/optimizing data).
*   **The "Daily Brief" (AI Generated):** 
    *   A semi-transparent card summarizing your day: *"You have 3 meetings. Based on your notes from yesterday, don't forget to ask Sarah about the project budget."*
*   **Recent Synapses:** 
    *   Horizontal scroll of the last 4 notes or files you interacted with, styled as high-tech "data chips."
*   **Expansion Slot (Future Feature):** 
    *   A reserved "Widget Area" for local weather, biometric stress levels, or focus-mode timers.

---

### **Screen 2: The Neural Vault (Knowledge Base)**
*Where all your documents, PDFs, and notes live.*

*   **The Semantic Search Bar:** 
    *   A permanent bar at the top. Placeholder text: *"Ask your brain anything..."*
*   **View Toggle:** 
    *   Switch between **"List"** (Classic), **"Gallery"** (Visual), and **"Cluster"** (AI groups files by topic automatically).
*   **Note Cards:** 
    *   Markdown-preview cards with "Auto-Tags" generated by MediaPipe (e.g., #Finance, #Ideas, #Urgent).
    *   **Action Swipe:** Swipe right to "Summarize," swipe left to "Delete."
*   **Expansion Slot:** 
    *   Reserved space for "Shared Brains" (Local encrypted p2p syncing with other devices).

---

### **Screen 3: The Synapse Chat (AI Agent)**
*The deep reasoning interface.*

*   **Atmosphere:** 
    *   Full-screen blurred background. No "bubbles"—text appears like a transcript for a cleaner professional look.
*   **Context Indicator:** 
    *   A small pill at the top: *"Reading: 3 Notes + 1 PDF"* (Clicking it lets you toggle which files the AI should "remember" for this specific chat).
*   **Streaming Text:** 
    *   Real-time local inference display. When the AI cites a note, it puts a small "Ref" icon that opens a preview on tap.
*   **Input Area:** 
    *   **Voice Waveform:** A real-time visualizer if using local Whisper for voice-to-text.
    *   **Action Palette:** A `+` icon that allows you to "Attach Note," "Take Photo," or "Run System Command."

---

### **Screen 4: The Graph View (Relationship Map)**
*The visual representation of your memory.*

*   **Interactive Web:** 
    *   Nodes (dots) representing notes. 
    *   **Heatmap:** Nodes glow brighter if they contain "Action Items" or are frequently accessed.
*   **Connection Lines:** 
    *   Solid lines for manual links. 
    *   **Neural Path (Dotted Lines):** AI-suggested connections (e.g., "These two notes are about different projects but use the same budget code").
*   **Time Slider:** 
    *   A slider at the bottom to see how your brain grew over the last month/year.
*   **Expansion Slot:** 
    *   "3D Mode" toggle (using Flutter’s 3D capabilities) to fly through your notes.

---

### **Screen 5: The Action Center (The "Doer")**
*Where the AI turns thoughts into reality.*

*   **Proposed Actions:** 
    *   A list of cards generated by the LLM: *"I found a task in 'Project X' note. Create a calendar event for Friday?"*
    *   **Buttons:** [Approve] [Edit] [Ignore].
*   **Automation Hub:** 
    *   List of active "Local Agents" (e.g., "Automatically summarize every PDF I download," "Silence phone when I am writing notes").
*   **Expansion Slot:** 
    *   "Third-Party Plugins" (e.g., local hooks for HomeAssistant or specific engineering tools).

---

### **Screen 6: Sensory Input (Capture)**
*The "Camera" and "Microphone" view.*

*   **OCR Viewfinder:** 
    *   Real-time highlighting of text in the camera view. When a phone number or date is detected, a futuristic "HUD" element pops up next to it.
*   **Audio Journaling:** 
    *   A massive, glowing record button. As you speak, the AI extracts "Entities" (People, Places, Dates) in real-time on the screen.

---

### **Upcoming Feature Hooks (The "Expansion Slots")**

1.  **"Mind Mirror" (Biometric Integration):** A screen that tracks your focus/productivity based on how you interact with your notes.
2.  **"Time Machine":** A local version of version control—reverting a note to how it looked 3 days ago.
3.  **"Ghost Mode":** An encrypted "Incognito" vault within the brain that requires secondary biometric auth.
4.  **"Peripheral Vision":** A desktop/tablet companion layout that works via local Wi-Fi.

### **UI Design Specs for Developer:**
*   **Main Font:** *Geist Mono* (Titles) / *Inter* (Body).
*   **Colors:** 
    *   Background: `#0A0A0B` (Obsidian).
    *   Accent: `#3D5AFE` (Electric Blue).
    *   Glass: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)` with `BackdropFilter(sigma: 15)`.
*   **Haptics:** Heavy use of "Light Impact" haptics when the AI finishes a sentence.

**This layout ensures that as we add "Action Agents" or "Multi-model" capabilities, the app doesn't feel cluttered—it just feels like your brain is getting more powerful.**
